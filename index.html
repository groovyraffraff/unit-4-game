Crystals Gems Collector!


You will be given a random number at the start of each game.

There are four Crystal Gems below. By clicking on a Crystal Gem you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, but you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each Crystal Gem is hidden from you until you click on it.

The values of the Crystal Gems will be different every game.
